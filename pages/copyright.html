Permissions & Copyright

We are open about where this content comes from and why we share it.

This platform exists in an area that many will rightly question. We have thought carefully about this, and we want to be as transparent as possible about our approach, what permissions we hold, and where we acknowledge uncertainty.

Who we are

A non-profit, open-source charity project

01

No money is made from this content

Improving Muslim does not earn advertising revenue, subscription fees, or any form of payment from the videos hosted here. There are no ads attached to the content we serve. The platform is funded voluntarily through donations from Muslims who want to see it exist.

02

Built as sadaqah for the ummah

This project is a charitable contribution to the Muslim community. The goal is to make beneficial Islamic lectures more accessible and watchable, without the distracting recommendations and harmful advertisements that come with standard video platforms. The code is open source and free for anyone to inspect or build upon.

03

Why we serve videos directly rather than embedding YouTube

Videos are served through a private CDN rather than as embedded YouTube players. This is specifically to remove ads, algorithm-driven recommendations, and distracting content that appear inside the YouTube player itself. We do not claim ownership of any content. Original links, speaker pages, and full attribution are provided throughout the platform.

Permitted
