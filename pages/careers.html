How we build

Product principles

01

Serve intention

Every feature should help a learner start, continue, or complete beneficial study without pulling them into distraction.

02

Keep it simple

We are not chasing social-media complexity. The core experience is series, episodes, progress, captions, and calm discovery.

03

Respect trust

Users come here to avoid ads, pop-ups, algorithmic traps, and unwanted recommendations. That trust has to shape the product.
