Private by default

Sign in or create an account

An account is optional. You can use Improving Muslim normally with progress stored only on this device. To keep your progress and saved lectures synced across devices, you will need to sign in.

Sync progress

Use Google for cross-device sync

Google handles authentication. Improving Muslim does not store your email, name, or profile photo in the learning-data database. Your watch history and saved items are synced to your Firebase account ID.

The same Google button signs you in or creates your account when you use it for the first time. Your local progress will be merged with any progress already synced to that account.

Continue without an account

You are signed in. Your learning progress can now sync across devices.

What stays local

Guest mode remains available

Without sign-in, your progress stays in this browser's local storage. It does not leave this device, but it also will not automatically appear on your other devices.
