Settings

Improving Muslim saves watch progress in your browser's local storage so you can resume lectures and see what you have already watched. Sign in with Google to sync your progress, saved items, and streaks across all your devices.

Sync

Account

Sign in with Google to sync your watch progress, saved items, and streaks across all your devices. You can continue without an account - everything stays saved locally on this device. Leaderboard sharing is optional and only publishes your display name and streak summary.

Sign in or create account

Your progress, saved items, and streaks are synced to this account across all your devices. We sync private learning data to your Firebase account ID, not your email address. Public leaderboard sharing stays optional.
