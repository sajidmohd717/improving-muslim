Privacy

Your study habits are your business, not ours.

This platform has no ads, no analytics trackers, and nothing to sell. This page explains plainly what data exists, where it lives, and how to remove it.

Guest mode

Without sign-in, everything stays on your device

01

What is stored locally

Watch progress, saved items, theme preference, and player settings are kept in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device and are not visible to us or to anyone else.

02

How to remove it

The Settings page lets you reset watch history and saved items on the current device at any time. Clearing your browser data does the same.

Signed-in accounts

If you sign in with Google

01

What we receive

Google Sign-In shares your name, email address, and profile photo with the site, along with a unique account identifier. We do not receive your Google password or any other Google account data.

02

What we store in the cloud

Your watch progress and saved items are synced to Cloud Firestore (a Google Firebase service) so they follow you across devices. Security rules ensure each account can only read and write its own data.

03

How to delete your data

Signing out stops all syncing immediately. To have your synced progress and account data permanently deleted, email contact@improvingmuslim.com from the address you signed in with and we will remove it.

Third parties

Services this site relies on

01

Video delivery

Videos are served from a Cloudflare-backed CDN. Like any web server, it sees your IP address in order to deliver the video. We do not use it to track viewing behaviour.

02

Fonts and sign-in

Fonts load from Google Fonts, and sign-in uses Google Firebase Authentication. Both are Google services and receive standard connection information such as your IP address when they load.

03

Feedback and error reports

The feedback form sends your message through FormSubmit to our contact inbox — your email address is included only if you choose to provide it. If a page crashes or a video fails to load, an automatic report with the page URL and browser version is sent the same way so we can fix it. These reports contain no name, email, or account information.

Questions

Contact

For any privacy question or data deletion request, email contact@improvingmuslim.com or use the feedback form.
