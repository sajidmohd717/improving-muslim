Partnerships

Reach Muslims who are already here to learn.

Improving Muslim is for focused Islamic long-form content. We are interested in partners who respect the mission: no distracting recommendations, no pop-ups, no attention traps, and no unwanted ads interrupting beneficial study.

Who this is for

Possible partners

Islamic creators

Collaborate on awareness, feedback, content organization, or directing audiences to a calmer viewing flow.

Muslim businesses

Explore ethical visibility to an audience interested in Islamic learning, without intrusive ad formats.

Masjids and institutes

Organize beneficial lecture series in a simple platform that helps learners continue without distraction.

Charities and projects

Reach Muslims in a context that is more aligned with worship, learning, and meaningful action.

Ground rules

What partnership should protect

01

No interruptive ad experience

Partnerships should not recreate the exact distractions this platform exists to avoid.

02

Audience fit matters

Any future promotion should be relevant, respectful, and useful to Muslims interested in learning.

03

Feedback is welcome

Early partners can help shape the product, especially around trust, content quality, and user experience.
